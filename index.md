# Welcome to Asgard.chat

**Asgard.chat** offers hosted **Matrix moderation bots** for everyone who needs them but cant host it themselves

## What We Offer

- **Hosted [Draupnir](https://github.com/the-draupnir-project/Draupnir)**  
  

- **Hosted [Meowlnir](https://github.com/maunium/meowlnir)** (coming soon)  



## Why moderationbots?

Whether you’re running a small chatroom or a large community with many rooms, spammers will eventually discover your rooms and start spamming.  
**Asgard.chat** gives you the tools to moderate effectively via powerfull bots without the headaches of self-hosting.

For details on how to request and set up your hosted bot, please follow the step-by-step guides in the **sidebar menu**. Start with **“Getting started”** under your chosen bot.
